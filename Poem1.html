Thak Chuka Hoon

by Vaibhav Sharma 01, May, 2025 
Thak chuka hoon zindagi ki bhaag-daud se,
Khud se bhi har roz ki is jaud se.
Sapne the anmol, par waqt ke saude mein,
Har khushi bik gayi kisi aur ke mauke mein.

Har baar asvikriti ka tamacha pada,
Har dafa dil ne bas khud se wada kiya.
"Agli baar jeetenge," socha har haar pe,
Par har baar kuch chhoot gaya sansaar ke pyaar pe.

Taaliyon ki goonj mein apna sannata chhupa,
Har jeet ke jashn mein, main chup-chap tha khud se juda.
Raaton ko poochhta hoon — kya kami reh gayi?
Kismat ke is khel mein meri baari kyun keh gayi?

Phir bhi subah uthta hoon, dil ko samjha ke,
“Abhi khatam nahi hua,” khud se keh ke.
Ek din meri kahani bhi misaal banegi,
Jo aaj hans rahe hain, meri taal pe tali bajegi.

Shiv khud kar rahe hain kuch khaas tayyari,
Mere liye likhi hai unhone ek anmol kahani.
Yeh waqt sirf ek kasauti hai, ek aag ka dariya hai,
Main us sankalp ki shakti hoon, jise Shiva ne swayam agni mein tapaya hai.
